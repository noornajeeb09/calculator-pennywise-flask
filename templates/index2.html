Credit Cards vs. Debit Cards

Which one is right for you?

Practice, early and often, with a credit or debit card can help you form healthy money habits as you get older.

Every year, more and more adolescents aged 8-13 are beginning their financial journeys with debit and credit cards. However, itâ€™s important to consider and understand the differences between each one to know which one is better for you!

Credit Cards: rely on credit that you pay back to the bank to make purchases
  • Loan from your card issuer
  • Can yield very high interest rates
  • May have a yearly fee
  • Rewards like points, miles, cash back

Debit Cards: rely on your money that you have saved in the bank
  • Money comes from your bank account
  • No repayment needed; just spend what you have
  • Usually no fees
  • Usually no rewards
